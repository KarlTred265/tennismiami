Miami Tennis Club

Miami Tennis club is one of the USA's most popular clubs with professional staff that offers the best in tennis training.

  • We offer our members optional training in all exercises.
  • We offer optional matches and tournaments on weekends.
  • We also offer admission to the world's elite in tennis for those who want to become professionals.

Our Tennis Plan is to practice as a tennis player with different matches and training to reach a higher level.

About Our History

Miami Tennis Club has been around since 2001 and has since had several talented members that we have trained who have become professionals today. We have a large network of different trainers and good training with good possibilities that you who choose us will also become a professional.

About our Tennis Global

We have several members who compete at an international level and will be with this year 2023 in the us open mixed and our own Miami open cup. We will be happy to take you who have the talent and desire to these levels that these players are at.
